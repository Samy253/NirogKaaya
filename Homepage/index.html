What is NirogKaaya ?

Access to quality medical care in rural India remains one of the most pressing challenges of our time. While over 65% of India's population lives in villages, a majority of doctors, hospitals, and modern healthcare infrastructure are concentrated in urban centers. Villagers often travel miles for basic treatment, rely on under-equipped health sub-centers, or turn to unverified practices due to lack of awareness.
